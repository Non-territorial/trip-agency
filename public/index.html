INTRO >

TRIP Agency offers travel experiences in the form of exhibitions, set in a single location. TRIP Agency will turn your stay into a live art experience, in an absolutely unprecedented way.

At the time of arrival, guests will receive the detailed program of the event. TRIP Agency will facilitate tours in the sorrounding areas, but there will be no leading assistance.

TRIP Agency is committed to support art projects whose nature and scale exceed the limits normally available within the traditional museum or gallery.

GUEST INFORMATION >

The TRIPS are scheduled to take place during the spring and autumn seasons, each lasting 4 days and 3 nights.

Bookings will be open for one month. To stay informed about when bookings become available, please fill out the BOOK form on our website to join the whitelist.

Kindly note that requests cannot be made over the phone. When submitting your request, ensure you include the number of guests in your party, as TRIPS have limited space (averagely from 08 to 40).

RESERVATIONS & CANCELLATIONS >

RESERVATIONS

Reservations are limited to one room for two people and cover the entire package. Requests must include the number of participants and the chosen date. In the event that there is no availability due to a shortage of places, we will offer the possibility of being placed on our waiting list for the following season. 

Once a date has been secured through our correspondence, we will send a reservation and payment link which must be completed within the time allotted (5 days) to confirm the hold. Reservations are nonrefundable unless we are able to re-book the reserved spots. 

CANCELLATIONS

If a guest must cancel the reservation, please email i@trip-agency.net as soon as possible. We will make a good faith effort to rebook the visit with another party. If we can successfully rebook, guest fees will be refunded to the original party.

Please note that your reservation is non-refundable unless we can rebook your visit with a new party. If you need to cancel, notify us as soon as possible so we can begin this process.

FEES >

FEES vary depending on multiple factors, they range between 3.500 and 10.000 EUR.

Once you have secured a date, you will receive an email with instructions for payment and confirmation.

Your reservation is confirmed only upon our receipt of your reservation form and payment in full within five days of receiving your hold email. We cannot accept payment upon your arrival at the location. 

If you need to cancel a booking after you have submitted payment, please do it as soon as possible. We can refund you for your canceled trip only if we are able to re-book your trip date for another guest regardless of the reason for the cancellation.

ACCOMMODATION >

TRIP Agency hosts its events in private residencies, villas or boutique hotels.

The journey expenses cover the full stay. All meals are included, but are not mandatory. Guests are free to choose to dine on their own, according their likings. If guests have any special dietary requirements, they are asked to inform us in due time.  

ARRIVAL & DEPARTURE >

TRIP AGENCY provides transportation from and to airport. All guests should arrive the first day of TRIP, before 2 p.m.

You will receive meeting point instructions upon confirmation of your reservation. Daytime trips and short visits cannot be accommodated. Guests who are intending to prolong their stay beyond the TRIP Agency event should inform us at the time of booking, so that we are able to provide assistance.

It is important to arrange your travel plans to meet this schedule, as off-schedule trips cannot be accommodated. If guests will be arriving at different times (day before or after) they will have to personally arrange transportation to the location. 

CLIMATE CONDITIONS >

Climate conditions vary depending on the geography of the location. Upon confirming your reservation, we will provide all the necessary details and tips to help you arrive prepared for the expected climate.

SOCIAL MEDIA RESTRICTIONS >

In keeping with the nature of the event we are offering, the use of smartphones and social media is prohibited in the exhibition's context, which is protected by copyright. We gently ask our guests to avoid the use of the phone altogether, limiting it to the moments spent in the room and to urgent issues only.

Images of the exhibitions may be published with written consent from TRIP Agency.
